Where Does the World's Whiskey Come From?

Beginner Aggregated


The world's whiskey is produced primarily from a few regions:

  • Scotland: Over 120 active distilleries, famous for 'Scotch' whiskey
  • United States: World's primary Bourbon producer
  • Canada: Interchangeable with 'Rye Whiskey'

Which Individual Expressions of Each Region has the Best Value?

Advanced Ratings Price Individual Expressions


Grouped by region, we see the relative value of each expression of whiskey in that region given by the area it takes.

The value is calculated by Average Rating / Average Price. Hence, a larger area denotes a better value.

A few noteworthy expressions:

  • Wild Turkey 81 is the best value Bourbon
  • Finlaggan Old Reserve is the best value for Islay Scotch
  • Te Bheag is the best value blended, and best value overall
  • Canadian and Irish has the best general value of all regions

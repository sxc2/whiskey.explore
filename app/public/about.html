Our goal is to help whiskey connoisseurs to explore whiskeys from the perspective of different data attributes and share interesting facts over neat scotch at a bar.

We also want to help whiskey beginners get acquainted with the different whiskeys the world has to offer - an expression that's a particularly good value, prefered flavor notes, etc.
